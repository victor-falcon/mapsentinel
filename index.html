The platform that lets consulates track and assist their citizens abroad during large-scale emergencies like:

  • Pandemics.
  • Wars.
  • Political/Social crises.
  • Terrorism Attacks.
  • Natural disasters.
  • Individual emergencies.

For Users:

Automatic alert feed provided by different highly reputable government agencies directly to the user's app in real-time and completely free.

Ability to directly contact your consulate in the country you are in, receive information on safe zones, advice, timelines, news, humanitarian flights, local police, and medical and psychological remote assistance.

For Embassies and Consulates:

Map Sentinel is a SaaS platform designed to serve embassies and consulates, providing diplomatic authorities with the ability to:

  • Track fellow citizens in a real-time map.
  • Guide users to safe locations.
  • Plan and communicate next steps.
  • Maintain an open channel allowing individually or bulk messages.
  • Manage groupings, humanitarian flights, humanitarian aid delivery, etc.

Worldwide allies

Our mission is to increase the chances of successful emergency management for citizens abroad by empowering consulate and embassies, providing direct, efficient, and immediate communication, even without Wi-Fi or a data plan when the technology allows it.
